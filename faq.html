General Questions

Is ImResizer really free?

Yes, ImResizer is completely free to use. No hidden charges, no watermarks, no registration required. All tools are 100% free forever. We believe essential tools should be accessible to everyone, especially students and job aspirants.

Is it safe to upload my images?

100% safe. All image processing happens in your browser. Your images never leave your device. We never see, store, or have access to your files. This is the core of our privacy-first approach. You can use our tools with complete confidence even for sensitive documents.

Do I need to create an account?

No account needed. ImResizer works instantly without any registration. Just upload your image, make adjustments, and download. No email, no password, no personal information required.

Do you store my files?

No, we never store any files. All processing happens in your browser's memory. Once you close the page or refresh, everything is gone. We cannot recover any files, so please download your processed images before leaving the page.

What file formats are supported?

Different tools support different formats:

  • Image Compressor: JPG, PNG, WEBP
  • Signature Maker: Draw (any), Type (any), Upload (JPG, PNG)
  • Passport Photo Maker: JPG, PNG
  • PDF Converter: PDF, JPG, PNG, WEBP

Image Compressor & Resizer

How to compress image to 20KB?

Upload your image to our main compressor tool, enter '20' in the Target KB field, and click Convert. Our algorithm automatically adjusts quality to achieve the exact file size while maintaining the best possible quality. The same process works for 50KB, 100KB, or any target size.

How to resize image in centimeters?

Use our main image resizer. Select 'cm' from the unit dropdown, enter your desired dimensions in centimeters, and the tool automatically calculates the required pixels at 300 DPI for print quality. You can also use mm, inches, or pixels.

What is DPI and why is it important?

DPI (Dots Per Inch) determines print quality. Screen images are 72 DPI, but printed photos require 300 DPI for sharp results. Our tools maintain 300 DPI minimum when you work in cm or mm, ensuring your photos look great when printed for exams, passports, or visas.

How to maintain aspect ratio?

Check the "Maintain Aspect Ratio" box below the width/height inputs. When enabled, changing one dimension automatically adjusts the other to keep your image proportions correct, preventing stretching or distortion.

Passport Photo Maker

What are the passport photo requirements for India?

Indian passport photo requirements: Size 35x45 mm (2x2 inch equivalent), white background, face should occupy 70-80% of the photo, neutral expression, both eyes open, no glasses, no headgear (except religious). Use our Passport Photo Maker for perfect results.

What size is US visa photo?

US visa photo size is 2x2 inches (51x51 mm). Head height should be 1-1 3/8 inches (25-35 mm) from chin to crown. Background must be white or off-white. Our tool has a preset for US visa photos.

Can I change background color?

Yes! Our Passport Photo Maker lets you change background to white, light blue, light gray, or any custom color using the color picker. This is useful for different country requirements.

How to print multiple passport photos on one page?

After creating your photo in the Passport Photo Maker, use the "Print Multiple Copies" section. Select paper size (4x6, A4, or 5x7) and gap between photos. Click "Generate Print Sheet" to download a ready-to-print page with multiple copies automatically arranged.

Signature Maker

How to create a digital signature?

Use our Signature Maker. You have three options:

  1. Draw: Use mouse or touch to draw your signature
  2. Type: Type your name and choose from various fonts
  3. Upload: Upload an existing signature image
Then adjust color, size, and download as PNG (transparent) or JPG.

What fonts are available for typed signatures?

Our Signature Maker includes professional fonts like Arial, Brush Script, Courier New, Georgia, Times New Roman, Verdana, Comic Sans, Trebuchet MS, Pacifico (handwritten), and Great Vibes (calligraphy).

Should I download as PNG or JPG?

PNG supports transparent background – ideal for overlaying on documents. JPG has white background and smaller file size. For most official uses, PNG is recommended as it looks professional without a white box around your signature.

PDF Converter

How to convert JPG to PDF?

Use our PDF Converter. Select "Image to PDF" mode, upload your JPG files (you can add multiple), choose page size (A4, Letter, etc.), orientation, margin, and quality. Click "Convert to PDF" and download your PDF with all images combined.

Can I merge multiple PDFs?

Yes, select "Merge PDF" mode, upload your PDF files (they'll be merged in the order you add them), and click "Merge PDFs". Download your combined PDF instantly.

How to extract images from PDF?

Select "PDF to Images" mode, upload your PDF, choose output format (JPG or PNG), quality, and DPI. Click "Extract Images" to get a ZIP file containing each page as a separate image.

Technical Issues

Why is my image not uploading?

Common reasons:

  • File format not supported (use JPG, PNG, or WEBP)
  • File too large (try under 20MB)
  • Browser compatibility (update to latest Chrome, Firefox, or Edge)
If problems persist, try a different browser or contact us.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes! All ImResizer tools are fully responsive and work on smartphones and tablets. You can draw signatures with your finger, upload photos from your gallery, and download files directly to your device.

What is the maximum file size?

While there's no hard limit, very large files (over 50MB) may process slowly depending on your device's memory and processing power. For best performance, keep files under 20MB. If you need to process larger files, try reducing resolution first.

Exam Photo Requirements

SSC photo requirements?

SSC (CGL, CHSL, MTS) requirements:

  • Photo: 3.5 x 4.5 cm, 20-50KB, JPG format
  • Signature: 4.0 x 2.0 cm, 10-20KB, JPG format
  • White background, name and date should be visible below photo
Use our main compressor for size and our Signature Maker for signature.

UPSC photo requirements?

UPSC Civil Services requirements: Photo and signature both 350x350 pixels minimum, 20-300KB file size, JPG format. Use our main resizer with 'px' unit to set exact dimensions.

IBPS banking photo requirements?

IBPS (Banking) requirements:

  • Photo: 4.5 x 3.5 cm, 20-50KB
  • Signature: 140 x 60 px, 10-20KB
Both in JPG format.
