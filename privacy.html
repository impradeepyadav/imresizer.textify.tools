Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 01, 2026

🔒 Key Point: ImResizer is a 100% client-side application. This means your photos are processed entirely in your browser and never uploaded to our servers. We cannot see, access, or store your images.

1. Our Privacy-First Approach

At ImResizer, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. That's why we built our tool to work entirely within your browser. You don't need to create an account, log in, or trust us with your personal data.

2. Client-Side Processing

ImResizer uses HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript to process images directly on your device. When you upload an image:

  • The file stays on your computer
  • All processing happens in your browser's memory
  • Your image is never transmitted over the internet
  • Once you close the tab, all data is permanently deleted

3. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
  • User accounts or login credentials
  • Uploaded images or documents
  • Payment or billing information

We may collect anonymous usage data:

  • Internet Protocol (IP) address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Date and time of visit
  • Referring website
  • Basic page interaction data

This information is used only for analytics, improving our service, and detecting technical issues. It cannot be linked to you personally.

4. Cookies

ImResizer uses minimal cookies for essential functions and analytics:

  • Essential cookies: For basic website functionality
  • Analytics cookies: To understand how users interact with our site (via Google Analytics)
  • Advertising cookies: If you see ads, they may use cookies to show relevant content

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect some non-essential features.

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate our website:

  • Google Analytics: For anonymous traffic analysis
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements
  • GitHub Pages: For hosting our website
  • Cloudflare: For DNS and security

These services have their own privacy policies and may use cookies. You can learn more about Google's practices at https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

6. Data Security

While no method of internet transmission is 100% secure, we take reasonable measures to protect our website:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Regular security updates
  • Minimal data collection
  • Client-side processing eliminates the risk of data breaches

7. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Because we don't collect personal data, there is typically no information to access, delete, or export. However, if you have questions about privacy, you can contact us at any time.

California residents (CCPA): You have the right to request disclosure of personal data collected. As we collect no personal data, there is nothing to disclose.

European Union residents (GDPR): You have rights to access, rectify, erase, and restrict processing of your data. Since we store no personal data, these rights don't apply to stored information, but you can still contact us with questions.

8. Children's Privacy

ImResizer does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The updated version will be posted here with a revised date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: support@imresizer.com
  • Response time: 24-48 hours

By using ImResizer, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this privacy policy.
