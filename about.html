About ImResizer

Last Updated: February 2026

Welcome to ImResizer, a free online image resizing and compression tool built specifically for students, job aspirants, and professionals who need to prepare photos for government exams, visa applications, and official documents.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to help you avoid photo rejection in your important applications. Every year, thousands of applications get rejected due to incorrect image size, wrong file format, or oversized files. We built ImResizer to solve this problem once and for all.

Why We Built ImResizer

As students ourselves, we struggled with complex photo requirements for exams like SSC, UPSC, IBPS, and RRB. Most photo editors are either too complicated, cost money, or don't support exact dimensions in centimeters. We wanted a tool that is:

  • Simple: Upload, adjust, download – that's it.
  • Accurate: Exact dimensions in cm, mm, inches, and pixels.
  • Secure: Your photos never leave your device.
  • Free: Forever free, no hidden charges.

Key Features

  • Compress to Exact KB: Target specific file sizes like 20KB, 50KB, or 100KB.
  • Multiple Units: Resize in centimeters, millimeters, inches, or pixels.
  • Exam Presets: Ready-made sizes for SSC, UPSC, IBPS, GATE, and more.
  • International Presets: US Visa, UK Passport, Schengen, Japan, Canada, China.
  • Name & Date Overlay: Add applicant details as required by SSC.
  • PDF Export: Create PDF documents with exact print dimensions.
  • Printable Sheets: Generate multiple copies on a single page.
  • 100% Client-Side: Your photos stay on your computer. We never see them.

Our Technology

ImResizer is built using modern web technologies that run entirely in your browser. We use HTML5 Canvas for image processing, Cropper.js for precise cropping, and jsPDF for PDF generation. All processing happens locally on your device. No servers, no uploads, no privacy concerns.

Our Team

ImResizer is a small team of developers passionate about creating useful, privacy-focused utilities for students and professionals. We believe that essential tools should be free, accessible, and secure for everyone.
