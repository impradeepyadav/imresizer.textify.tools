Why Compress Photo to KB?

Government exam forms like SSC, UPSC, IBPS, RRB, and State PSCs require photographs within specific file size limits - typically 20KB to 50KB. Our Photo to KB converter helps you achieve exact file sizes while maintaining excellent quality for your application.

Common Exam Photo Size Requirements

  • SSC CGL/CHSL/MTS: 20KB to 50KB (JPG format)
  • UPSC Civil Services: 20KB to 300KB (JPG/PNG)
  • Banking (IBPS/SBI/RBI): 20KB to 50KB
  • Railway RRB: 15KB to 40KB
  • NEET/AIIMS: 10KB to 200KB
  • JEE Main/Advanced: 10KB to 100KB

How to Compress Photo to 20KB, 50KB, or 100KB

  1. Upload your photo: Click the upload area and select your image (JPG, PNG, WEBP).
  2. Select target size: Choose 20KB, 50KB, 100KB or enter a custom KB value.
  3. Choose format: Select JPG for photos or PNG if you need transparency.
  4. Compress: Click the compress button - our algorithm automatically adjusts quality to hit your target.
  5. Download: Save your perfectly compressed photo ready for upload.

Pro Tips for Best Results

  • Start with a clear, well-lit photo (minimum 300x300 pixels)
  • For signatures, PNG format often gives better results
  • If file is too large initially, try reducing dimensions first
  • Always preview the compressed photo to ensure clarity
